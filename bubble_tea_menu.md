# Chinese Bubble Tea Menu

## Signature Bubble Teas
- **Pearl Paradise**: Milk tea with black pearls, topped with fresh sliced strawberries and a drizzle of honey syrup.
- **Matcha Magic**: Matcha green tea with black pearls, garnished with whipped cream and a sprinkle of matcha powder.
- **Tropical Mango**: Mango milk tea with black pearls, served with a slice of fresh mango and a mint leaf.
- **Classic Milk Tea**: Traditional milk tea with black pearls, finished with a touch of caramel syrup.

## Specialties
- **Lychee Lemonade**: Lychee milk tea with black pearls, served with a slice of lemon and a sprig of mint.
- **Passionfruit Pomegranate**: Passionfruit milk tea with black pearls, topped with pomegranate seeds and a splash of lime juice.

## Small Bites
- **Fried Shrimp Rolls**: Crispy shrimp wrapped in rice paper, served with a sweet and sour sauce.
- **Vegetable Spring Rolls**: Fresh vegetables wrapped in rice paper, served with a peanut dipping sauce.
- **Fried Rice Bites**: Mini portions of fragrant fried rice, served with a side of soy sauce.
- **Sticky Rice Balls**: Sweet rice balls filled with red bean paste, served with a light soy sauce glaze.
